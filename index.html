About Us

What Do We Do?

The days of waiting in long lines are gone. The Queue app combines in-person and phone booking online in one convenient system. It functions as a virtual reception desk, receiving appointment requests from clients, locating their appointment among existing bookings, updating them on waiting times, and finally contacting them for their appointment turn. Clients can track the countdown to their appointment using their smartphones, allowing them to run other errands while waiting and saving time. This digitilized queue management eliminates queues and busy waiting rooms, and ensures social distancing, optimizing safety and comfort for both customers and the staff.

Usage

How To Use It

For Provider

Install the Queue App from our Marketplace. For the provider app, first, register using a valid email account. Open the app and customize your setting like Opening time, closing time, Active or close days, and average queue time. You'll be provided with a QR code. On scanning the code, you'll jump into the scheduling page where you can access the queue length and next arrivals. A provider can cancel the booking anytime.

For Client

For the client app, again register yourself on the app using your email. After registering, scan the QR code given by your provider. You can easily select which services you want to assign Queue App booking to, by adding them to your provider list. Once they are added, you can see the current queue details. Request "add me to the queue" and you'll be updated with your estimated appointment time. You can leave the queue anytime you want. It is also possible to book yourself with multiple providers at the same time.

Variants

There are two Variants of Queue App

Provider Variant

Even the broad smile of a receptionist cannot mask the fact that waiting times and long queues negatively affect customer satisfaction. For a provider, the Queue app serves as a mobile-integrated queue management system to minimize waiting times and optimize the customers' flow, allowing your customers to virtually join a queue before visiting. After registering on the app, the provider gets the QR code that is provided to clients to let them book the appointment and know the estimated time and turn.

Client Variant

Clients use their smartphones to access a variety of services such as hospitals, banks, pharmacies, supermarkets, government organizations, retail stores, and much more. A waiting time estimate is provided immediately after they request an appointment. Customers scan QR codes with their phones to jump right into the booking process. An appointment ticket number is sent to clients, and they can track the countdown and queue number to their session turn from their smartphones, allowing them the freedom to wait elsewhere or run other errands. An app notification alerts a client when their appointment is due. Real-time updates on queue lengths and waiting time also help to schedule changes like cancellations..
